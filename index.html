About
Me

I am a multi-disciplinary designer with a background in media, communication, and cultural studies. My current role at a university think tank in London involves a dynamic mix of collaborating with academics and their partners from diverse sectors. I handle design work for various projects; producing publications and creating digital assets to promote our executive education program, public lectures, and off-the-record think-tank events.

I am adept at all the core Adobe CC programmes: Illustrator, InDesign and Photoshop, specifically. I also like using Figma and Visual Studio Code for my online courses on UX/UI Design and Interactive Data Visualisation using HTML, CSS, SVG, and D3.js. For fun, I am also tinkering with the p5js libraries, Touch Designer,Spark AR and various generative AI tools online.

I enjoy bringing research to life visually and find genuine satisfaction when the project team happily gets their message across. Eager to contribute my skills to a dynamic and forward-thinking studio or startup, I aspire to shape impactful projects that align with my growing conmmitment towards creating a more inclusive and sustainable future for everyone.
